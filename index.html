Welcome to Wings of Discovery

Helping archaeologists discover the past with modern technology

About Us

Wings of Discovery is a project created by kids who are passionate about technology and history! We believe that archaeology is super cool, but we also know that archaeologists have to walk through huge fields for hours and hours looking for ancient artifacts and sites. That's really tiring!

So we thought: what if we could use drones and artificial intelligence to help them? That way, archaeologists can spend less time walking and more time making amazing discoveries!

ðŸŽ¯ Our Mission

To reduce field walking for archaeologists by using drone technology and AI-powered image detection.

We want to make archaeology easier, faster, and more exciting by combining modern technology with the study of ancient history. Our goal is to help archaeologists find important sites without having to walk through endless fields in all kinds of weather.

What We Do

We use two amazing technologies to help archaeologists:

  • Drone Mapping: Flying drones over large areas to take detailed photos and create maps from the sky
  • AI Image Detection: Using artificial intelligence to analyze the drone photos and spot things that humans might miss
