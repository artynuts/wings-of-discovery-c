What is AI Image Detection?

AI (Artificial Intelligence) Image Detection is like teaching a computer to "see" and recognize things in pictures, just like you can recognize your friends in a photo! We train computers to look at thousands of drone photos and spot patterns, shapes, and colors that might show where ancient sites are hidden.

The cool thing about AI is that it can look at hundreds of photos in minutes, finding things that would take humans days or weeks to spot. It never gets tired, never blinks, and can see tiny differences in color and texture that our eyes might miss!
